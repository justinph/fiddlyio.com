  • Biography: Justin Heideman

    I build websites and interactive experiences, from visual design to back-end programming. I'm most experienced with web publishing (blogs, news sites, etc), but have also interest and experience in data visualization and mapping.

    I am fluent in HTML, CSS, and javascript, as well as CSS-preprocessors like LESS and SASS. I prefer to build my javascript around jQuery and RequireJS, and know a lot about optomizing websites for speedy client side performance. On the server, I like using python and Django, but often get stuck using php and WordPress.
